Screen Saver 12 / 13








If you want apples iOS 13 screen saver app you need a jailbroken device and the following two tweaks:
Apps Manager - Repo
Ext3nder - Repo

Now follow this instruction:
Download the iOS 13 backup or iOS 12 backup and the Screen Saver ipa. Then open Ext3nder and install the ipa. After this you just have to insert the backup in apps manager and restore the app with the backup. That's all!

If you have problems you can contact JBP or me on twitter. Make sure you follow us on twitter.
